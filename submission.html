Guidelines

Submission guidelines.

15 August 2026Abstract submission deadline

Abstract submissions are due by 15 August 2026.

29 August 2026Acceptance notification

Committee Reviewer notification before 29 August 2026.

30 October 2026Full paper deadline

Full paper submission deadline is 30 October 2026.

Abstract length

Abstracts should be between 250 - 300 words.

Full paper length

Full papers should be between 4,000 - 6,000 words.

Language

Papers must be written in English.

Original work only

All submissions must be original, unpublished and not under consideration elsewhere.

Referencing style

Use APA referencing style.

Review process

Submissions will undergo peer review and editorial evaluation.

Publication opportunities

Selected papers will be considered for publication.

All submissions will be reviewed before publication consideration.

MyCITE-indexed publication

Selected papers may be considered for MyCITE-indexed publication opportunities.

SCOPUS publication

Selected papers may be considered for SCOPUS publication opportunities.

30 October 2026Full Paper Submission Deadline
