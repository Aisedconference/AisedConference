Conference theme Harnessing AI, Empowering Innovation, Advancing Sustainable Futures Through Entrepreneurship and Diplomacy.

Researchers, academics, postgraduate students, policymakers, industry practitioners and professionals are invited to submit original and unpublished papers related to the conference theme and thematic tracks.

Thematic tracks

AI for Sustainable Development

Sustainable Entrepreneurship and Innovation

AI and Digital Transformation in Business

Diplomacy in the AI Era

Policy, Governance and Ethics of AI

Green Economy and Circular Business Models

Education, Skills and AI for Future Work

Smart Cities and Sustainable Societies

AI for Health, Well-being and Human Security

AI in Technology and Creative Industry

Paper types
  • Conceptual papers
  • Empirical research papers
  • Case studies
  • Policy analyses
  • Innovation and industry papers
  • Applied research and best practice papers
Guidelines
  • Abstracts: 250 - 300 words
  • Full papers: 4,000 - 6,000 words
  • Language: English
  • Original, unpublished work only
  • Peer-review and editorial evaluation
  • Authors should align papers with the conference thematic tracks
  • Selected papers may be considered for indexed publication opportunities
  • Paper templates, review guidelines and publication updates will be announced through official conference channels
